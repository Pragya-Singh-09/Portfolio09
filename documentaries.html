  • Documentary: Conservation Chronicles of Okhla Bird Sanctuary

    A journey through the scenic Okhla Bird Sanctuary in Noida, India. This documentary showcases the diverse bird species found within the sanctuary and includes an insightful interview with the Divisional Forest Officer (DFO), who shares the challenges and triumphs of preserving this natural habitat amidst urbanization.

  • Documantary on NGO: Nirantar Prayaas Welfare Foundation

    This documentary provides an inside look at the Nirantar Prayaas Welfare Foundation, an NGO dedicated to educating children. The film captures the organization’s unique approach of combining traditional learning with creative activities like dancing, singing, and drawing, showing how the foundation creates a fun and supportive environment for kids to thrive.
